"A Texas/National Gaited Horse Experience"

Barefoot or Lite Shod Quality, Sane Pleasure Tennessee Walking Horses For Sale With Extensive Camping and Trail Riding Experience, Natural Horsemanship Training

We practice Natural Horsemanship Training on all of our horses. We train the horse to THINK instead of just REACT. And we LOVE doing it all BAREFOOT!

We produce barefoot and keg and lite shod pleasure horses with extensive traveling, camping and trail experience. (This is our lifestyle and we travel a lot, training our own riding horses and then selling them, starting all over with a less experienced horse and polishing it.)

We raise them, we buy some, polish and resell, and we take consignments. We sell quality weanlings, yearlings, brood stock, trail horses and a few which have crossed from the trail to the show ring with great success.
